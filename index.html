Where to stay

Anywhere either downtown or in North, Northeast, or Southeast Portland will be great, though nearer the ceremony will be best. Airbnb is great for staying in a neighborhood, so try to find something close to the wedding (700 NE Dekum St). If you prefer a hotel, some ideas:

  • North and Northeast Portland
    • Viking Motel: Inexpensive. Great access to the light rail. Our house is 15 minutes walking to the west, and the wedding is about 25 minutes walking to the east.
    • McMenamins Kennedy School: We hear they're booked :(. Once an elementary school, now a hotel with a restaurant, two bars, a second-run movie theatre, and a soaking pool. A bunch of New England folks have already booked rooms here, so keep bugging them in case other rooms become available.
  • Downtown
    • McMenamins Crystal Hotel: McMenamins are fun. This one has a bar and restaurant, music venue, and a soaking pool. Near Powells Books.
    • The Ace Hotel: Trendy place in West End, with great food (Clyde Common) and coffee (Stumptown) on the first floor. Also near Powells!
  • Southeast
    • The Jupiter Hotel: Next to the Doug Fir Lounge, a neat smaller music venue. The neighborhood around it has food and breweries and music. Portland stuff. It's nice.
